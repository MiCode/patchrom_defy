Portable Wi-Fi hotspot

You can turn your phone into a portable Wi-Fi hotspot, to share your phone's internet connection with one or more computers or other devices
  • When your phone is acting as a Wi-Fi hotspot, you will be unable to use your phone's applications to access the internet via its Wi-Fi connection

  • You configure the hotspot in Wi-Fi hotspot settings

  • For more information, visit http://www.android.com/tether#wifi
